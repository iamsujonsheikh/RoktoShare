### 6. Data **Requirements**

6.1 **Rgister**
- name
- email
- phone
- password
- role (donor/recipient/admin)

6.2 **Login**
- email / phone
- password

6.3 **Donor Profile Full Data**
- name
- email
- phone
- password
- role (donor/recipient)
- bloodGroup
- district
- dateOfBirth
- gender
- availability (true/false)
- occupation (optional)
- profilePhoto
- lastDonationDate
- cooldownEndDate
- eligibilityStatus (true/false)
- donationCount
- weightEligible (true/false)  —  ☑️ I confirm my weight is at least 50kg
- isVerified (true/false)
- isDeleted (true/false)
- status (active/ban/suspand)

6.4 **Recipient Profile Full Data**
- name
- email
- phone
- password
- role (donor/recipient)
- district
- dateOfBirth (optional)
- gender
- profilePhoto
- totalRequestCount
- isVerified (true/false)
- status (active/ban/suspand)

6.5 **Blood Request Data - Recipient**
- requestId
- recipientId
- bloodGroup
- description
- urgencyLevel (Emergency / Scheduled)
- **district**
- expiresAt
- requestStatus (pending/accepted/fulfilled/expired/canceled)

- emergencyBadge (true/false)

6.6 **Donation**
